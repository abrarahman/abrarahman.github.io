Why I Love Italian Cuisine

Italian cooking emphasizes quality ingredients and simple preparation that lets flavors shine. What makes it special:

  • Regional diversity from Northern to Southern Italy
  • Emphasis on seasonality and freshness
  • Perfect balance of flavors in every dish
  • Rich culinary traditions passed down through generations

Favorite Recipe: Simple Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente
  2. Meanwhile, gently heat olive oil and add sliced garlic and red pepper
  3. Cook until garlic is golden (not brown)
  4. Drain pasta, reserving 1/4 cup cooking water
  5. Toss pasta with oil mixture, adding reserved water if needed
  6. Finish with fresh parsley and optional Parmesan
