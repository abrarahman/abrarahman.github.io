My Favorite Travel Destinations

Discover the beauty and wonder of America's National Parks, my favorite places to explore.

Top National Parks

Yellowstone National Park

America's first national park, featuring incredible geothermal features, diverse wildlife, and stunning landscapes.

Best Time to Visit: June to September

Grand Canyon National Park

A breathtaking natural wonder carved by the Colorado River, offering spectacular vistas and hiking trails.

Best Time to Visit: March to May and September to November

Yosemite National Park

Famous for its towering waterfalls, ancient sequoias, and iconic valley views including Half Dome and El Capitan.

Best Time to Visit: May and September

Zion National Park

Known for its red cliffs, emerald pools, and unique slot canyons including the famous Narrows.

Best Time to Visit: April to May and September to October

Why I Love National Parks

National Parks offer natural beauty and outdoor adventure that rejuvenate the spirit. What makes them special:

  • Preservation of America's most spectacular landscapes
  • Opportunities to disconnect from technology and reconnect with nature
  • Diverse ecosystems to explore, from deserts to mountains to forests
  • Rich wildlife viewing opportunities
  • Educational experiences about geology, ecology, and conservation
